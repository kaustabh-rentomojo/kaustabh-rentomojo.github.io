 PAYMENT AND BILLING

Is there any advance or deposit required? What would be my deposit amount?

A refundable security deposit mentioned below every item and will refunded post completion of tenure.

When do I make my first rental payment?

Our rental payments are postpaid. The invoice will be raised by Rentomojo on the 1st day of every month and the due date for payment shall be the 10th day of the month ( “Due Date”).For eg- The product is delivered on 16th of this month, then the Invoice will be generated on 01st of the following month on pro- rata basis from 16 - last day of the month. Payment made beyond the Due Date shall incur a late fee

Can I pay upfront for my rental tenure?

Yes, you may. Please write to us at JO@RENTOMOJO.COM  for the same. Alternatively, you can also call in at 1860-121-1233

When can I expect my subsequent invoices?

The invoice will be raised by Rentomojo on the 1st day of every month and the due date for payment shall be the 10th day of the month ( “Due Date”).

When am I expected to make the payment for the monthly invoices?

You'll have a 10 day period after receiving the invoice in which you are expected to clear the invoice amount.Payment made beyond the Due Date shall incur a late fee.

What happens if I make a late payment?

Late fees shall be levied on the rental due amount. The late fees shall be applied three times each month; 10% on the 11th day of the month, another 5% on the 18th day of the month and another 5% on 25th day of the month, the late fees shall be applied only on the pending amount.

What payment modes can be used to complete the payment?

You can opt for Auto Debit payments by linking your Credit Card or Signing up for NACH for auto - debit through netbanking. Alternatively, we have online payment options through Debit/ Credit Card, Netbanking, UPI and wallet Payments.

AUTO PAY

What is Auto-Pay?

With Rentomojo Auto-Pay, you can securely set up standing instructions or link your credit card for automatic payments towards your Rentomojo subscription.

What are the benefits of Auto-Pay?

Just like Netflix or Saavn, Rentomojo is a subscription service. Auto-Pay, eliminates the inconvenience of monthly payments. You can simply set up a standing instruction or link your credit card and we’ll take care of the rest.

How does Autopay work?

When you sign up for Auto-Pay, Furlenco will automatically request payment from your authorized credit card, debit card or any other payment instrument whenever an invoice is raised towards your subscription. It’s easy, secure and super-convenient.

How do I register for Auto-Pay

You can register for Autopay by visiting the Profile Tab  in your subscription account and authorizing automatic payments towards your Rentomojo subscription.
